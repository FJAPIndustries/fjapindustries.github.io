Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Welcome to Airfall (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use the Airfall game.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Camera Access

Airfall requests access to your device’s camera for the sole purpose of generating background themes within the game. The app does not access or read your camera roll, gallery, or stored photos.

1.2 Device Sensors

Airfall uses your device’s motion sensors (such as accelerometer and gyroscope) to detect movement and provide input control for gameplay.

1.3 Other Information

Airfall does not collect, store, or transmit personal data such as your name, email, or location. No account or login is required to play.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Camera: To create in-game background themes based on real-world visuals.
  • Motion Sensors: To control game movement and improve gameplay responsiveness.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

Airfall does not share your personal data with third parties. The app does not use analytics, advertising networks, or external SDKs that collect user data.

4. Data Storage & Security

Airfall does not store or retain any camera images, sensor data, or personal information on any server. Any data processed is only used temporarily on your device during gameplay.

5. Permissions

Airfall requires the following permissions:

  • Camera Access: Required to generate background themes.
  • Motion Sensor Access: Required for game controls.

You may disable these permissions at any time through your device settings, but doing so may prevent the game from functioning correctly.

6. Children’s Privacy

Airfall is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: your-email@example.com.

By using Airfall, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
