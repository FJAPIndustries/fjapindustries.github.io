1. Information We Collect and Process

We may collect and process the following categories of information:

1.1 Information You Provide Voluntarily

  • Contact Information: Email address, support requests.
  • Profile Data: Username, preferences entered in the app.
  • Transaction Information: Purchase receipts, in‑app purchase confirmation (handled by app stores).
  • Feedback/Support Messages: Messages you send us via support or email.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version, language settings.
  • Usage Analytics: Interaction with our Services, screens viewed, time spent in the app.
  • Diagnostic & Crash Data: Performance data, error logs, crash reports.
  • IP Address & General Location: Used for analytics and security purposes. Precise geographic location is not collected unless explicitly enabled for certain features in specific apps.
  • Cookies & Similar Technologies: In web contexts, to improve your experience and prevent fraud.

1.3 Third‑Party Information

If you link or log in through third‑party services (such as social accounts), we may receive profile information that you permit the third party to share, including usernames, profile pictures, and identifiers. The information received depends on your social privacy settings.

2. How We Collect Information About You

We collect information through:

  • Direct interactions (support, feedback forms)
  • Automatic data collection when you use our Services
  • Third‑party integrations and analytics services
  • Logs generated by your use of the app or website

3. How We Use the Information We Collect

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • Service Operation: To operate, maintain, and improve our Services.
  • Personalization: To remember preferences and deliver tailored experiences.
  • Communications: To respond to inquiries, send updates, alerts, and support messages.
  • Analytics: To analyze usage patterns and trends to improve Services.
  • Marketing: With your consent, to send promotional content.
  • Security & Compliance: To detect fraud, enforce policies, and comply with applicable law.

4. Sharing of Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as needed with:

  • Affiliates: Subsidiaries, parent companies, or entities under common control.
  • Service Providers: Analytics, crash reporting, cloud services.
  • App Store/Payment Processors: Entities that process transactions (e.g., Google, Apple).
  • Advertising Partners: For marketing where permitted and with consent.
  • Legal Authorities: To comply with legal obligations or protect rights.

5. How to Exercise Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access and receive a copy of your data.
  • Correct inaccuracies.
  • Delete your personal data.
  • Restrict or object to processing.

To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

6. Data Deletion & Retention

Your data is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes described or required by law. Local data stored on your device remains until you delete it, uninstall the app, or remove data via settings.
