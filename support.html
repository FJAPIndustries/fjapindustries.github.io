Contact Guidelines

When contacting support, please provide as much detail as possible to help us resolve your issue efficiently:

  • Which app you are contacting us about
  • Your device type and operating system
  • Steps to reproduce the issue (if reporting a bug)
  • Any screenshots or error messages

Response Time

We strive to respond to all support inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. Please note that response times may be longer during weekends or public holidays.
